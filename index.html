Travel and Transportation

No one we know lives in Jensen Beach, so everyone will be traveling. Thank you so much, your presence means the world to us!

West Palm Beach International Airport (airport code: PBI) is the closest major airport, about an hour away by car. You may want to consider renting a car as well, especially if you are going to want to do some independent exploring. All of the major car rental companies have hubs (and corresponding shuttles) near the airport.
